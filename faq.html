General Questions

MyDigiSign.AI is an AI-powered digital signage platform that transforms any screen into a smart display. It allows you to create, manage, and display content including images, videos, slideshows, and live TV channels. The platform uses artificial intelligence to automate content management, scheduling, and provide performance insights.

MyDigiSign.AI is designed for businesses of all sizes, from small retail stores to large corporations. It's perfect for restaurants, retail stores, offices, waiting rooms, lobbies, and any business that wants to display dynamic content on screens. No technical expertise is required - our AI handles the complexity.

MyDigiSign.AI is available on Android TV, Fire TV, Samsung SmartTV, and LG SmartTV. You can download the app from the respective app stores for each platform. The app works on any screen connected to these devices, making it easy to transform existing displays into smart signage.

No special hardware is required! MyDigiSign.AI works with any TV or display that can connect to the supported devices (Android TV, Fire TV, Samsung SmartTV, or LG SmartTV). If you already have a smart TV or streaming device, you can start using MyDigiSign.AI immediately.

Our AI technology handles content optimization, scheduling suggestions, and performance analytics. It can suggest the best times to display certain content, analyze what performs best with your audience, and automatically manage content rotation. The AI learns from your usage patterns to provide increasingly better recommendations.
