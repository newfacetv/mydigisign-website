Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up MyDigiSign.AI?

Setting up MyDigiSign.AI is simple! Download the app on your device, create a free account, and follow our 3-minute setup guide. No technical skills required.

What devices are supported?

MyDigiSign.AI works on Android TV, Fire TV, Samsung SmartTV, and LG SmartTV. We're constantly adding support for more devices.

Is the free plan really free?

Yes! Our free plan includes 1 device, 1 screen, and access to free live TV channels. You can upgrade anytime to remove ads and add more devices.

How does the AI work?

Our AI automatically manages content scheduling, performance optimization, and provides insights to help you maximize engagement with your displays.

What types of content can I display?

You can display images, videos, slideshows, and live TV channels. We support JPG, PNG, GIF images and MP4, MOV, AVI videos. The platform automatically optimizes your content for optimal display.

How do I get technical support?

We offer multiple support channels including email support, comprehensive documentation, and video tutorials. Paid plan users receive priority support with faster response times.
